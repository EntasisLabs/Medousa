# Medousa 0.1.0

**Turn chaotic life into stone.**

First public release of Medousa — a permanent AI workspace that lives on your devices. It remembers what you tell it, verifies what it tells you, and keeps working even when you close the window.

**Local-first. No Medousa subscription. Answers you can trace.**

---

## Download

```bash
curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/EntasisLabs/Medousa/main/scripts/install-app.sh | bash
```

That script reads the release CDN bootstrap and opens the right installer for your OS.

- **[Bootstrap JSON](https://releases.entasislabs.com/medousa/stable/installer-bootstrap.json)** — same source of truth (Mac · Windows · Linux)
- Getting started: [docs/guides/getting-started.md](https://github.com/EntasisLabs/Medousa/blob/v0.1.0/docs/guides/getting-started.md)
- Phone companion (iOS & Android): pair from **Settings → Phone**

Engine / CLI / adapter packages also attach to this GitHub Release for self-host and repair installs.

## Highlights

### Chat that keeps working
Durable turns on a local engine — close the window, sleep the laptop, come back to finished work. Thinking traces when the model provides them. Memory and identity that sharpen over time.

### Vault, Library, and Presentations
Markdown notes with wikilinks and tags. Finished HTML guides and sandboxed presentations land in Library — readable and exportable, not buried in scrollback.

### Calendar (personal `.ics`)
A first-class **Calendar** surface for your schedule — Day / Week / Month, import & export, and agent tools so Medousa can list and update events with you.

### Automations & Capabilities
Schedule check-ins and working sessions. Flows, scripts, and specialists. Medousa can keep going while you sleep.

### Phone as a portal
Pair iOS or Android to a desktop running the engine (QR). Your phone is the window — not a second brain host.

### Channels you already use
Telegram, Discord, Slack, WhatsApp — reach the same brain from chat apps you already live in.

### Peers & nearby workshops
Discover trusted engines on your LAN (or over the tunnel), share turns, keep an inbox between your machines.

### Canvas & custom views
Ask Medousa to pin your own pages — studios, dashboards, braindumps — in the sidebar.

### Private brain (optional)
Run fully offline with a local model package, or bring your own cloud keys. Your hardware, your rules.

## Install path (Home-first)

1. Run `install-app.sh` (or download from the bootstrap JSON)
2. Finish the welcome flow (offline brain, BYO key, or Ollama)
3. Chat
4. Add optional packages later from **Settings → Packages** (offline brain, adapters, CLI, MCP gateway)

The **Medousa Installer** remains an advanced / repair escape hatch — most people never need it first.
